Chicken Recipe

Chicken is a versatile, tender white meat enjoyed worldwide for its mild flavor and adaptability. It can be roasted, grilled, fried, or simmered in sauces, making it a staple in countless cuisines.

Ingredients required:

  • Chicken pieces (breast, thighs, or whole chicken)
  • Oil or butter
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Garlic (fresh or powder)
  • Onion (optional)
  • Herbs (like thyme, rosemary, or parsley)
  • Lemon or vinegar (for flavor and tenderness)

Method for making chicken:

  • For Simple Pan-Fry:

    1. Marinate chicken with salt, pepper, garlic, and lemon juice. Let it rest for at least 20 minutes.
    2. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
    3. Place chicken pieces and cook for 6–8 minutes per side (time depends on thickness), until golden brown and fully cooked (juices run clear).
    4. Garnish with herbs and serve hot.

  • For Simple Roast:

    1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
    2. Rub chicken pieces with oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and lemon juice.
    3. Place on a baking tray and roast for 35–40 minutes, turning halfway, until skin is crispy and meat is cooked through.
    4. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.
