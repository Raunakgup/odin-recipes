Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with a rich filling, then baking until hot and golden.

Ingredients required:

  • For the Meat Sauce (Ragù):

    • Olive oil or butter
    • Onion (finely chopped)
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Ground beef and/or pork (sometimes sausage is used)
    • Tomato paste or crushed tomatoes
    • Tomato sauce or canned tomatoes
    • Red wine (optional, for depth of flavor)
    • Herbs: basil, oregano, thyme, bay leaf
    • Salt and black pepper

  • For the Cheese Layers:

    • Ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese as a substitute)
    • Grated Parmesan cheese
    • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
    • Egg (mixed with ricotta for binding)

  • For the Pasta:

    • Lasagna noodles (dried, fresh, or no-boil type)

  • For White Sauce (optional, common in traditional recipes):

    • Béchamel sauce (made with butter, flour, and milk)

  • For Assembly and Baking:

    • Fresh parsley or basil (chopped, for garnish)
    • Extra Parmesan for topping

Method for making lasagna:

  1. Prepare the meat sauce

    1. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add onions, cook until soft.
    2. Stir in garlic, then add ground meat. Cook until browned.
    3. Add tomato paste, stir well, then pour in crushed tomatoes (and wine if using).
    4. Season with oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper.
    5. Simmer on low for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Prepare the cheese mixture

    1. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and parsley.
    2. Mix until smooth and set aside.

  3. Cook the pasta (if not using no-boil)

    1. Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente.
    2. Drain and lay flat on a clean kitchen towel to prevent sticking.

  4. Assemble the lasagna

    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a baking dish (about 9×13 inches), spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    3. Add a layer of noodles (3–4 sheets).
    4. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture, then spoon meat sauce over it.
    5. Sprinkle mozzarella on top.
    6. Repeat layers (noodles → ricotta → sauce → mozzarella) until everything is used.
    7. Finish with noodles on top, covered with meat sauce and extra mozzarella + Parmesan.

  5. Bake

    1. Cover with foil (tent it so cheese doesn’t stick).
    2. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove foil.
    3. Bake uncovered for another 20–25 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
    4. Let it rest 10–15 minutes before slicing (this helps it set).
